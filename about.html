About Pratik

Senior Product Designer. 10 years in the craft. 8 years in fintech. Based in Mumbai, India.

The story
I didn't start in fintech.
I started in animation.

Somewhere between turning 3D renders into brand stories and crafting motion graphics for fast-moving agencies, I discovered what I really loved: making complex things feel simple.

That led me to product design — and eventually to NIUM, where I've spent the last 8 years designing financial tools used by businesses across the globe. Whether it's a cross-border payment dashboard or a high-stakes compliance flow, my job is the same: take something intimidating and make it feel obvious.

I work end-to-end. From the first user interview to the final developer handoff — I stay close to the problem, close to the people, and don't step away until the thing ships well.
