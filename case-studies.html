Remittance UX Redesign
Lead Designer · NIUM · 2021–2022
Cross-border payment flows were failing under the weight of compliance rules, multi-currency edge cases, and users operating across 100+ jurisdictions. I redesigned the end-to-end flow — from initiation to confirmation — making complexity invisible to the people who matter most.
Reduced average task completion time. Support tickets down.

Design Systems
NIUM Design System — Built from Zero
Sole Owner · NIUM · 2019–Present
NIUM had no shared design language. Every squad was building its own components, creating inconsistency at scale. I built the design system from the ground up: tokens, components, interaction patterns, accessibility standards, and the contribution model other teams now build on.
Adopted across all product squads. WCAG AA compliant throughout.

KYC Onboarding Flow
Lead Designer · NIUM · 2020–2021
For B2B clients, onboarding meant navigating document uploads, identity verification, and regulatory checks across different jurisdictions. I redesigned the flow to feel less like bureaucracy and more like a product — clear progress, plain language, no surprises.
Improved completion rates. Reduced time-to-active for new clients.

Card Issuance Management Dashboard
Lead Designer · NIUM · 2022–2023
NIUM's card issuance product lets businesses issue physical and virtual cards at scale. I designed the management dashboard to handle complex data hierarchies, spend controls, and real-time transaction monitoring — without overwhelming the people responsible for it.

v.
PaymentsFX
FX Rate Display and Conversion UX
Lead Designer · NIUM · 2023–2024
Currency conversion is inherently anxiety-inducing — rates change, fees are hidden, and users lose trust fast. I redesigned NIUM's FX display to make rates transparent, timing clear, and the conversion step feel confident rather than uncertain.
Increased conversion completion. Reduced FX-related support queries.
